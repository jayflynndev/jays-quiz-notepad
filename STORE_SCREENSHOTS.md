# Store Screenshot Guide

Use the real app screens for store screenshots. No screenshot-only route is
needed or exposed in the app.

## Prepare the app

1. Use a test quiz with a clear title, valid date, and valid YouTube video.
2. Prepare one completed answer sheet with a believable score and one answer
   sheet in progress.
3. Keep names and answers suitable for public display.
4. Capture from a native development or preview build once EAS access is
   available. Expo Go is useful for layout checks, but its fallback ad banner
   should not appear in final store artwork.
5. Capture the current required phone sizes from Apple and Google templates at
   release time. Do not stretch one screenshot across different aspect ratios.

## Suggested story

1. **Home - Next Quiz**
   Show the branded header and featured next quiz card.
   Caption: "Never lose your quiz answers again."

2. **Answer Sheet - Video and first answers**
   Show the sticky video, quiz title, score panel, and start of Round 1.
   Caption: "Watch and answer in one place."

3. **Answer Sheet - Marking**
   Show a mix of correct, incorrect, and unmarked answers with a useful score.
   Caption: "Mark your answers and see your score."

4. **Home or Past Quizzes - Completed scores**
   Show completed and in-progress states together.
   Caption: "Keep track of recent quizzes."

5. **Settings - Quiz reminders**
   Show the reminders section with at least one reminder enabled.
   Caption: "Get quiz reminders."

Keep captions outside the app screenshot when composing store artwork. The app
itself should remain unchanged and readable without promotional overlays.
