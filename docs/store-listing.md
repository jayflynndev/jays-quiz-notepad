# Jay's Quiz store submission pack

Prepared for version 1.0.0.

## 1. Product Overview

### App name

Jay's Quiz

### Tagline options

1. Watch, answer and score as you play.
2. Your answer sheet for Jay's Virtual Quiz.
3. Quiz night, all in one place.
4. Play along. Mark answers. Track scores.
5. The simple companion for virtual quiz night.

### Positioning

Jay's Quiz is the companion app for Jay's Virtual Quiz, bringing the quiz video,
answer sheet, self-marking and score together in one clear mobile experience.
Players can open the latest quiz, save answers locally as they play, mark their
sheet and revisit recent results. It also establishes a focused mobile home for
quiz experiences provided through QuizHub services, without requiring an
account or cloud storage.

### Full description

Make quiz night easier with Jay's Quiz, the official companion app for Jay's
Virtual Quiz.

Watch the quiz and complete your answer sheet in one place. Your answers save
automatically on your device, so you can concentrate on the questions. When the
answers are revealed, mark each response and see your score clearly.

With Jay's Quiz you can:

- See the next scheduled quiz and recent quiz nights.
- Watch the quiz video alongside your answer sheet.
- Enter answers round by round with automatic local saving.
- Mark answers as correct or incorrect.
- View your score as soon as marking is complete.
- Reopen locally saved quiz sheets and past results.
- Set optional reminders for upcoming quiz nights.
- Keep the screen awake while you play, if you choose.

No account is required. Quiz answers, marks and scores are stored locally on
your device and can be cleared from Settings.

Jay's Quiz contains banner advertising and uses embedded YouTube video for quiz
playback. An internet connection is required to load quiz information, videos
and advertising.

## 4. Screenshot Plan

Use real release-build screens with representative quiz information. Do not
show test ads, personal notifications, debug overlays, placeholder support
details or identifiable device information. Keep the same quiz and score across
the full screenshot sequence.

### iPhone checklist

- [ ] **Home: Next Quiz**
  Show the branded header, Next Quiz card with a readable title/date/time, status
  and Open Answer Sheet action. Demonstrates immediate access to the next event.
- [ ] **Answer sheet: video and answers**
  Show the sticky video area, one complete round heading and several populated
  answer fields. Demonstrates watching and answering in one place.
- [ ] **Marking and score**
  Show a mix of clearly marked correct and incorrect answers with the prominent
  score visible. Demonstrates self-marking and instant score calculation.
- [ ] **Past quizzes**
  Show multiple saved quiz entries with played states, dates and at least one
  score. Demonstrates local quiz history and reopening a sheet.
- [ ] **Settings and reminders**
  Show the organised reminder controls plus Privacy, About and Support links;
  avoid centring destructive data controls. Demonstrates optional reminders and
  transparent settings.

Capture the required App Store Connect iPhone sizes from the same approved
layout. Check the smallest supported iPhone for clipped controls and the largest
required size for excess empty space.

### Android checklist

- [ ] **Home: Next Quiz**
  Show the branded header and prominent Next Quiz card, including status and
  answer-sheet state. Demonstrates a focused starting point for quiz night.
- [ ] **Answer sheet: video and answers**
  Show the sticky video/header area and comfortably spaced answer inputs.
  Demonstrates continuous playback access while completing a sheet.
- [ ] **Marking and score**
  Show the marking controls in use and the score summary. Demonstrates the full
  answer-to-result workflow.
- [ ] **Past quizzes**
  Show saved quiz cards with human-readable dates, completion states and scores.
  Demonstrates easy access to previous locally saved sheets.
- [ ] **Settings and reminders**
  Show notification options, data information and the Privacy/About/Support
  group. Demonstrates user control and release transparency.

Capture phone screenshots at a Play-supported aspect ratio and resolution.
Verify status/navigation bars, consent UI and banner placement do not cover app
content. Use the same visual narrative and copy order as the iPhone set.
